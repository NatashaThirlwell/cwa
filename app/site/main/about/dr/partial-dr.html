Dr. Celeste Thirlwell B.Sc (neuro), MD, FRCPC

Dr. Celeste is the founder and director of the Cobalt Wellness Association.

Her keen interest regarding the nature of consciousness fuels her lifelong passion of optimizing the integration of physical and mental healthcare strategies.

As a neuropsychiatrist, sleep medicine specialist with interests in complementary cutting-edge healing modalities, she has developed SWAP, a comprehensive, neuroscience-based program that is unique in North America and the world for treating insomnia and complex sleep disorders with comorbid illnesses.

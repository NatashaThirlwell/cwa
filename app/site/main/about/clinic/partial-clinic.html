The Cobalt Clinic

CWA's Cobalt Clinic focuses on treating disorders that are bi-directionally related to sleep. We are dedicated to helping clients swap their maladaptive behavioural and psychological patterns affecting sleep, in order to optimized their physical and mental health. To do so, we empliment our Sleep Wake Awarness Program (SWAP) methodology by using cutting-edge, integrative health care treatments as well as providing educational seminars to allied health care professionals and clients.

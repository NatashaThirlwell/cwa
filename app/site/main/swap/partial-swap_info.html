Sleep Wake Awarness Program

SWAP is a multi-disciplinary, integrative health promotion program which conducts comprehensive evaluations of the co-morbid illness in patients and their bidirectional relationship with sleep. SWAP provides sleep and wake health education to patients to enhance compliance and encourage them to swap maladaptive behavioral and psychological patterns affecting sleep, therdy optimizing their physical and mental health.
